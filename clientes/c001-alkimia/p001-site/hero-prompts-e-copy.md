### PROMPT 2 — Serigrafia/DTF
Editorial black and white photography, studio or workshop lighting. Close-up of a silkscreen printing process: a squeegee or ink roller pressed against a fine mesh screen stretched over a wooden frame, with a black t-shirt visible beneath ready to receive the print. ### PROMPT 3 — Bordado + Oversized
Editorial fashion photography, black and white, natural or soft studio light. An oversized sweatshirt or hoodie worn on a model (torso only, no face needed), with a close-up detail of an embroidered chest patch or sleeve. The embroidery has texture and depth — thread raised above the fabric, tight stitching visible — but no legible letters or logos, only abstract or geometric stitch patterns. The garment is loose-fit with a ribbed collar. Clean white or light grey background. No text, no lettering anywhere in the image. High contrast, product-forward. Aspect ratio 3:2 horizontal.

### PROMPT 4 — Ecobag
Editorial lifestyle photography, black and white, natural light. A structured canvas tote bag (ecobag) resting on a rustic surface — a concrete ledge, a wooden bench, or a film set prop table. The bag is unfolded and slightly open, showing its volume and the weight of the cotton canvas. A subtle fabric tag is attached to one handle with no readable text. The environment suggests creative or production work (cables, equipment out of focus in background) without being literal. No text, no lettering, no logos visible anywhere in the image. Clean, high contrast, editorial feel. Aspect ratio 3:2 horizontal.
